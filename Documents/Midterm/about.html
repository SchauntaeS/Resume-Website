About Me

I am a dedicated college student pursuing a degree in computer programming while working full time. My journey in programming began in high school, igniting a passion for technology and problem-solving that continues to flourish.

In addition to academic and professional commitments, I volunteer with NeighborWorks Great Falls, contributing to community efforts by helping to build homes alongside 10 Homeowners. This hands-on experience has deepened my appreciation for craftsmanship and community service.

Looking ahead, I plan to start building my own house in the spring of 2025, blending my love for programming with practical skills learned through volunteering.

Outside of school and work, I enjoy a peaceful life surrounded by beloved cat, dog, and boyfriend of three years. As an outdoor enthusiast, I love hiking, swimming, and camping. In quieter moments you can find me immersed in movies and books or lending a helping hand to friends.

With a strong sense of community and a commitment to personal growth, I hope to embody the spirit of balancing ambition with a love for the simple joys of life.
